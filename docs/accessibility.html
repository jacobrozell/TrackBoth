TrackBoth is a habit and vice tracker for iPhone and iPad. Core screens are designed for clarity: readable typography, themed contrast, and support for common iOS accessibility settings.

Supported features

Dark Interface

TrackBoth includes multiple app themes and respects system light and dark mode.

  • In the app: Settings → Appearance → choose a theme.
  • Or use iOS Settings → Display & Brightness with your preferred appearance.

VoiceOver

Track metric rows, logging controls, tab navigation, and settings actions include accessibility labels and identifiers for assistive technologies.

  • iOS Settings → Accessibility → VoiceOver
  • Vice entries announce Avoided or Not Avoided rather than generic toggle states.

Larger Text (Dynamic Type)

Most screens use semantic text styles that scale with your system text size. Track and Settings adapt layouts at larger sizes; some chart summaries may require scrolling on smaller phones.

Contrast and non-color cues

Theme tokens are checked for sufficient contrast. Habit and vice status use text and icons—not color alone.

Known limitations

  • At the largest accessibility text sizes, some chart summaries may require scrolling on smaller phones.
  • TrackBoth does not include video content; Captions and Audio Descriptions do not apply.
