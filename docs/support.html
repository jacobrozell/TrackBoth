Common questions

How do I delete all my data?
Open Settings → Reset All Local Data → confirm. This permanently removes habits, entries, and goals on that device.

How do I back up or restore my data?
Open Settings → iCloud Backup to save to your private iCloud account, or use Export Data to save a JSON file you control.

Does the app require an account?
No. TrackBoth works offline with data stored on your device.

Does the app show ads?
No. TrackBoth is free and ad-free.

Where is my data stored?
On your iPhone or iPad. See our Privacy Policy for details on optional iCloud backup and export.
