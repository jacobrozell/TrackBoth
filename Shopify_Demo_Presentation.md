# Shopify Demo Presentation - Blue Orleans Jewelry

## Demo Agenda (45-60 minutes)

### 1. **Website Overview & Design** (10 minutes)
- **Current Status**: Store is in trial mode, needs to be unlocked for public access
- **Theme Customization**: Show current design and customization options
- **Mobile Responsiveness**: Demonstrate how it looks on mobile devices
- **Brand Consistency**: Ensure design matches Blue Orleans aesthetic

### 2. **Product Management** (10 minutes)
- **Adding Products**: Demonstrate product creation workflow
- **Product Images**: Show how to upload and organize jewelry photos
- **Inventory Management**: Basic stock tracking and variants
- **Product Categories**: Organizing by jewelry type (necklaces, earrings, etc.)

### 3. **Email Forms & Customer Communication** (8 minutes)
- **Contact Forms**: Show how customers can reach out for custom pieces
- **Newsletter Signup**: Email collection for marketing
- **Order Notifications**: Automated emails for order confirmations/shipping
- **Custom Inquiries**: Handling "Need a Customized Piece" requests

### 4. **Pricing & Promotions** (8 minutes)
- **Discount Codes**: Create and demonstrate promo codes
- **Seasonal Sales**: Setting up holiday promotions
- **Bulk Discounts**: Quantity-based pricing for wholesale
- **Free Shipping**: Threshold-based shipping promotions

### 5. **Point of Sale (POS) Opportunities** (7 minutes)
- **In-Person Sales**: Shopify POS for craft fairs, pop-ups
- **Inventory Sync**: Real-time inventory updates between online/offline
- **Payment Processing**: Credit cards, cash, mobile payments
- **Receipt Management**: Digital receipts and customer data collection

### 6. **Pricing Structure & Monthly Fees** (5 minutes)
- **Current Plan**: Basic Plan - $348/year ($29/month)
- **What's Included**: 
  - Online store
  - Unlimited products
  - 2 staff accounts
  - 24/7 support
  - POS Lite
- **Transaction Fees**: 2.9% + 30¢ per online transaction
- **Additional Costs**: Domain ($24/year), apps, themes

### 7. **Migration Process** (10 minutes)
- **Admin Role Transfer**: Moving from Jacob to Myra
- **Business Email Setup**: Switching to Myra's business email
- **Payment Information**: Updating billing and bank details
- **Domain Transfer**: Moving GoDaddy domain to Myra's account
- **Security Updates**: Password changes, 2FA setup

## Key Talking Points

### Why Shopify?
- **Ease of Use**: Intuitive interface for non-technical users
- **All-in-One**: Store, payments, inventory, marketing in one platform
- **Scalability**: Grows with the business
- **Support**: 24/7 customer service
- **Mobile-First**: Built for mobile commerce

### Revenue Opportunities
- **Online Sales**: Primary revenue stream
- **POS Sales**: Craft fairs, pop-up shops, in-person events
- **Custom Orders**: Higher-margin personalized pieces
- **Wholesale**: Bulk orders for retailers

### **PRIORITY Business Growth Strategies**
- **Customer Retention & Loyalty Programs**: VIP tiers, referral incentives, birthday discounts
- **Content Marketing & SEO**: Blog with jewelry care tips, styling guides, customer stories

### **Additional Revenue Streams** (Future Opportunities)
- **Subscription Boxes**: Monthly jewelry collections
- **Jewelry Care Products**: Cleaning kits, storage boxes
- **Gift Cards**: Prepaid cards for future purchases
- **Corporate Gifts**: Bulk orders for businesses/events
- **Jewelry Repair Services**: Restoration/repair services

### Cost-Benefit Analysis
- **Monthly Cost**: ~$29/month + transaction fees
- **Break-Even**: Just 1-2 jewelry sales per month
- **ROI**: Significant potential with proper marketing
